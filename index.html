01 · ABOUT

THE ARTIST

EST. 2014 MONROVIA → OTTAWA

Sound is my language.
Music is my mission.

Based in Ottawa, Canada, NOISEMAKERS is a Liberian songwriter, producer, musician and sound engineer dedicated to helping artists create timeless music.

From songwriting to final mastering, every production is crafted with creativity, precision and emotion. I believe every artist has a unique sonic fingerprint — my job is to help you discover it, amplify it, and share it with the world.

With over a decade of experience and collaborations across Africa, North America and Europe, I bring an international perspective to every session. Whether you're an emerging talent or an established name — let's make something unforgettable.
